1. Introduction
----------------
Super Side Scroller was programmed as a final project for Fundamentals of Computing II taught by 
Professor Scott Emrich in the Spring 2012 semester by:

	  Justin Bartlett, Computer Science, 2014
	  Jake Flynt, Computer Science, 2014
	  Eli Kloswick, Computer Science, 2014

The game was programmed in C++, using QT Creator, and is stored on Google Code at:

    	 http://code.google.com/p/side-scroller-project/

In the game's directory, upon compiling, there are N files:

   6 .h file(s)
   8 .cpp file(s)
   5 .o file(s)
   28 .png file(s)
   11 .wav file(s)
   9 .txt file(s)
   1 .pro file(s)
   1 Executable file(s)

2. Installation
----------------
Open a terminal window and go to the desired installation directory.  Check out the project from Google Code by typing:

svn checkout http://side-scroller-project.googlecode.com/svn/trunk/ side-scroller-project-read-only

To compile, type the following commands, one by one, in order:

   setenv QTDIR /usr/lib64/qt-3.3
   qmake-qt4 -project
   qmake-qt4
   make

The first three commands synthesize the necessary files for a QT executable application, including the Makefile.  The 'make' command compiles the application, as with any C++ program.

Typing ./side-scroller-project will run the application in a QT Window.


3. Game premise
----------------
The game is based on the Mario franchise and makes use of several NintendoÂ® images. 
Controlling the main character Mario, the user travels through levels, avoiding enemies such
as Goombas, while making his way to Princess Peach, held captive by Bowser.  Here, Mario will 
face Bowser in a final battle to save the princess.


4. Characters
--------------
   A. Mario...
	is depicted as a short, pudgy, Italian-American plumber who lives in the Mushroom Kingdom. He repeatedly stops the turtle-like villain Bowser's 	numerous plans to kidnap Princess Peach, destroy him and take over the kingdom. Mario also has other enemies and rivals, including Donkey Kong 		and Wario.  Mario can destroy goombas by jumping on them, and earn extra lives by collecting 1UP mushrooms.

   B. Princess Peach...
	is the Princess of the fictional Mushroom Kingdom and often plays the damsel in distress role within the series and is the lead female for the 		entire series.

   C. Bowser...
	is the leader and most powerful of the Turtle-like Koopa race and is Mario's greedy archnemesis.  Bowser is stronger than other enemies, and 		will take multiple hits to destroy.

   D. Goombas...
	look like shiitake, with bushy eyebrows and a pair of fangs sprouting from their lower jaw.  They are most often found walking about 		  aimlessly, only causing Mario harm.


5. Controls
------------
Menu:
ENTER - move to next stage
SPACEBAR - begin the game
H - help (print README to terminal)

In-game:
A - move left
D - move right
W - jump
L - skip a level (debug mode only)
